Primary action button. Quest Blue fill, white text, pill or md radius.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary">Start Quest</Button>
<Button variant="ghost">Sign Out</Button>
```

- `primary` — Quest Blue bg (`#4364F7`), white text. One clear action per screen.
- `ghost` — transparent, Slate Muted text, border for secondary/destructive actions.
- `disabled` — 40% opacity.
