1
Local Signal Rule
Indigo (#6366F1) appears on ≤8% of any screen. Vivid on white — restraint makes it feel earned.
2
Category Independence Rule
Category colors identify quest type only. Never mix with indigo. Never use for emphasis or status.
3
Glass-Over-Sky Rule
White surfaces on Saltwater Sky background — the contrast IS the elevation. No tinted cards.
4
Weight-Over-Size Rule
Jump font weight (700→800) before jumping size. Hierarchy from weight contrast, not decorative scale.
5
Flat-By-Default Rule
No shadows on resting list rows, section headers, or containers. Card Glass shadow for interactive cards only.
